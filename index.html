Our Mission

reconsult e.V. bridges the gap between business and sustainability. We are a passionate group of interdisciplinary students who are academically grounded, internationally minded, and 100% dedicated to sustainability. Every project we take on is based on one philosophy: that fresh perspectives and deep subject-matter knowledge can turn sustainability challenges into real business opportunities.

Our Vision

An exceptionally driven student consultancy, setting the standard in sustainability consulting — shaping the goals of the organizations we work with, and building a greener tomorrow, one project at a time.
